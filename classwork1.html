this is a list on how to make a sandwich

  1. get a plate
  2. get two slices of bread and put them on the plate
  3. get some lunchmeat
  4. get some mayo
  5. get some cheese
  6. get some mustard
  7. get a butterknife
  8. open the mayo
  9. smear some mayo on the knife
  10. smear a thin, even layer of mayo on one side of each slice of bread
  11. close the mayo
  12. open the lunchmeat container
  13. get two slices of lunchmeat
  14. put one slice of lunchmeat on each slice of bread
  15. close the lunchmeat container
  16. open the cheese package
  17. get one slice of cheese
  18. put the slice of cheese on one of the slices of bread
  19. open the mustard
  20. squirt a little bit of mustard on just one slice of bread
  21. put the slices together so that the mustard on one slice meets the lunchmeat on the other
  22. use the knife to diagonally cut the sandwich in half
